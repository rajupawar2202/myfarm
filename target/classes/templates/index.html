Pawar Farms - Panshet

Welcome to Organically grown Mango and Regional Fruits Farm

Nestled in the heart of the region, our farm began as a dream and has become a daily practice of care. We grow 175 organically managed mango trees and a selection of regional fruits, each tree nurtured from sapling to maturity. During the monsoon we protect young roots and manage drainage; in the peak of summer we shade, mulch, and irrigate thoughtfully to keep trees healthy and fruiting. We continuously refine our methods, experimenting with companion and side crops such as tur (pigeon pea) and watermelon to improve soil fertility, diversify harvests, and reduce risk. This is more than agriculture for us — it is a place to slow down, reconnect with nature, and give our children a chance to grow up close to the land.

Our farm is our dream in motion, and every season brings new lessons and quiet rewards.

Our Mission - AtmaNirbhar: Let most of the food items in our meal plate comes from our beloved farm. We are cultivating Mangoes, Guava, Jackfruit, Cashewnuts, Watermelons, Kokam, Lemon, Jamuns

Knowledge: Continuously learning agriculture, food forest technics from experts and local farmers. Our aim is to apply all knowledge everyday in farm

Also to assist new farmers or those who started food forest recently
